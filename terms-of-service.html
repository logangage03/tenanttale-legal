Scoring System

How Scores Work

TenantTale calculates scores on a 300–850 scale based on:

  • Verified feedback from connected parties
  • Self-reported profile information
  • Verification status of documents
  • Historical patterns of behavior

Score Limitations

Scores are informational tools, subject to change based on new data, not guarantees of future behavior, and based on available verified information.

Scores are NOT: consumer credit reports under FCRA, background checks, criminal history reports, or substitutes for proper tenant screening.
