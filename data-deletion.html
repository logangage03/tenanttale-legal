Data Deletion Instructions

How to delete your TenantTale account and personal data

You have the right to request deletion of your account and all associated personal data at any time. TenantTale provides two ways to do this: through the App or by email.

Option 1: Delete Through the App (Recommended)

1 Open Settings

Tap your profile icon, then tap Settings.

2 Go to Privacy & Data

Scroll down and tap Privacy & Data.

3 Tap “Delete My Account”

At the bottom of the screen, tap the Delete My Account button.

4 Confirm deletion

Review the summary of data that will be deleted, optionally provide a reason, then confirm by toggling the acknowledgement and tapping Delete My Account.

Option 2: Delete by Email

If you cannot access the App, send an email to privacy@tenanttale.com with the subject line “Account Deletion Request” and include the email address associated with your account. We will process your request within 5 business days and confirm via email.

What Happens After You Request Deletion

30
day grace period before permanent deletion

When you request account deletion, a 30-day grace period begins. During this time:

  • Your account is deactivated but your data is preserved.
  • You can cancel the deletion at any time by signing back in and going to Settings > Privacy & Data.
  • After 30 days, your data is permanently and irreversibly deleted.

Export Your Data Before Deleting

Before deleting your account, you can download a complete copy of your personal data:

  1. Go to Settings > Privacy & Data > Export My Data.
  2. Choose JSON (machine-readable) or PDF (human-readable) format.
  3. Save the exported file to your device.

We recommend exporting your data before requesting deletion, as it cannot be recovered after the 30-day grace period.

Third-Party Data

Deleting your TenantTale account removes your data from our systems. Data held by third-party authentication providers (Apple, Google, Facebook, Microsoft) is governed by their respective privacy policies. To revoke TenantTale’s access:

  • Apple: Settings > [Your Name] > Sign-In & Security > Sign in with Apple
  • Google: myaccount.google.com/permissions
  • Facebook: Settings > Apps and Websites
  • Microsoft: account.microsoft.com/privacy
