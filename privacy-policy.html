  1. Provide Services — Calculate and display tenant and landlord reliability scores, facilitate connections between tenants and landlords, process feedback and dispute resolution.
  2. Verify Information — Confirm user identity, verify property ownership, validate employment and income claims.
  3. Improve Our Services — Analyze usage patterns to enhance features, debug and fix technical issues, develop new features.
  4. Communicate With You — Send notifications about score changes, remind you of pending monthly inputs, notify you of connection requests.
  5. Ensure Security — Detect and prevent fraud, protect against unauthorized access, enforce our Terms of Service.

Your Rights and Choices

Access and Portability

You can export a complete copy of your personal data at any time from within the App (Settings > Privacy & Data > Export My Data) in JSON or PDF format. You can also view your full score calculation breakdown.

Correction

You can update your profile information at any time through the App.

Deletion

You may request deletion of your account and associated data by:

  • Using the “Delete Account” feature in the App (Settings > Privacy & Data > Delete My Account)
  • Emailing us at privacy@tenanttale.com

When you request deletion, a 30-day grace period begins during which you can cancel the request. After 30 days, your data is permanently deleted. Some information may be retained as required by law (see Data Retention below).

Consent Management

You can manage your data processing consents at any time (Settings > Privacy & Data > Manage Consents). Essential processing (score calculation, profile management, fraud prevention) cannot be disabled while your account is active. All optional processing—including payment history, feedback collection, verification data, communication preferences, score sharing, and rental matching—can be individually toggled on or off.

Opt-Out

  • Push notifications: Through your device settings
  • Marketing emails: Using the unsubscribe link in emails
  • Optional data processing: Through in-app consent controls

California Residents (CCPA/CPRA)

California residents have additional rights: the right to know what personal information is collected, the right to delete, the right to correct, the right to opt out of the sale of personal information, and the right to non-discrimination for exercising these rights. We do not sell your personal information.

EU/EEA Residents (GDPR)

If you are located in the EU or EEA, you have rights under the General Data Protection Regulation including the right of access (Art. 15), rectification (Art. 16), erasure (Art. 17), restriction of processing (Art. 18), data portability (Art. 20), and the right to object to processing (Art. 21). Our legal bases for processing are contractual necessity and consent. You may withdraw consent at any time without affecting the lawfulness of prior processing.

Tenant Screening (Background Checks)

Landlords may request FCRA-compliant background checks on applicants through the App via TransUnion SmartMove. Screening requires the tenant’s explicit written consent before any check is initiated. Results may include credit, criminal, and eviction history. If a landlord takes adverse action (e.g., denial) based on screening results, FCRA-required adverse action notices are generated through the App, including consumer rights information and CRA contact details.

Children’s Privacy

TenantTale is not intended for users under 18 years of age. We do not knowingly collect information from children. If we learn we have collected information from a child, we will delete it promptly.
